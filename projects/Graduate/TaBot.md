### Abstract

TaBot is a robotic table that can sense and interpret the subtle atmosphere of the conversation (e.g., duration, paces, tone of voice, word choice, and language patterns, etc.) and provide “ambient cues” to the individuals through non-verbal movements. This work was exhibited at Shenzhen Art Museum (New Venue).

### 摘要
TaBot 是一款能够感知并解读对话微妙氛围（如持续时间、节奏、语调、措辞及语言模式等）的机器人桌子，并通过非语言动作向用户提供“环境线索”。该作品曾在深圳美术馆（新馆）展出。
